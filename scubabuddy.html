Scuba Buddy

Scuba Buddy is a smart scuba diving mask that enables scuba divers to easily log their dive infomation as well as have the information in real time.Scuba Divers need a way to view their important infomation in real time, in one place because a lot of their information is currently gathered from different pieces of equiptment.
